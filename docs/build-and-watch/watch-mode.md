# Watch Mode

Watch Mode plays back your built pre-vis sequence directly in the UE viewport.

## Entering Watch Mode

Click the **Watch** button in the Director View toolbar. The side panels collapse and the viewport expands to give you the most screen space possible.

## Playing back

The built scene sequence opens automatically in UE's Sequencer. Press **Spacebar** to play. You'll see:

- Character blocking animation playing back
- Camera cuts switching between your assigned cameras
- Shot timing based on your script page lengths

## Exiting Watch Mode

Click **Exit Watch** in the Director View toolbar. GAS Tools will:

1. Close the sequence
2. Restore the Director View panels
3. Return to the mode you were in before (Blocking or Shot Edit)

## Tips for reviewing

- Use the Sequencer scrubber to jump to specific shots
- Press **Ctrl+Shift+P** to pilot a camera directly if you want to reframe on the fly
- The sequence playback reflects the current build — if you've made changes since the last build, rebuild first

:::tip
Watch Mode is for review only. Don't make edits while in Watch Mode — exit first, make changes, rebuild, then re-enter Watch Mode.
:::
