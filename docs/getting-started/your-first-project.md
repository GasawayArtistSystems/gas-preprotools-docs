# Your First Project

This page walks you through the end-to-end workflow for the first time.

## 1. Open the plugin

Click the **GAS Tools** button in the main UE toolbar to open the plugin window.

## 2. Create a new project

Click the **gear icon** (⚙️) in the toolbar to open Project Preferences and configure your project name, aspect ratio, and scene numbering style.

## 3. Import your script

Go to the **Script Tab** and click **Import FDX**. Select your Final Draft `.fdx` file. Your script will appear in the script panel with scene headings, action lines, and dialogue correctly formatted.

## 4. Generate page breaks

Once your script is loaded, click **Generate Page Breaks** to automatically divide your script into pages. You can drag page break markers to adjust them manually.

## 5. Start blocking

Double-click a scene in the scene list to open a blocking session. Your characters will appear as stand-in actors in the 3D viewport. Move them into position, record keyframes, and build out your scene.

## 6. Design your shots

In the Shot List panel, click **Add Shot** to create shot markers. Assign a camera and shot type (CU, MCU, WS, etc.) to each shot.

## 7. Build and watch

Click **Build Scene Sequence** to compile your blocking and shots into a UE Level Sequence. Then switch to **Watch Mode** to play it back.
