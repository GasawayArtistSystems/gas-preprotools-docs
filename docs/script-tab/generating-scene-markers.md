# Generating Scene Markers

Scene markers are automatically derived from your script's scene headings. Each scene heading becomes a scene entry in the scene list.

## How scenes are detected

When you import a script, GAS Tools reads every **Scene Heading** block and creates a corresponding scene entry. Scene numbers are assigned based on your Project Preferences (sequential numbering or Final Draft-style).

## Scene numbering styles

You can change how scenes are numbered in **Project Settings → Scene and Shot Numbering**. Options include:

- Sequential (1, 2, 3…)
- Final Draft style (1, 2, 2A, 3…)
- Custom start number

## Viewing your scenes

The scene list appears in the left panel of the **Director View**. Each scene shows its scene number and heading text.

## After generating scenes

Double-click any scene in the list to open a blocking session for that scene. See [Starting a Blocking Session](../blocking/starting-a-blocking-session).
