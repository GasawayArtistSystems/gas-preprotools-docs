# The Cast Panel

The cast panel shows the characters in the current scene. It appears on the right side of the Director View when you're in Blocking mode.

## What it shows

The cast panel lists every character who appears in the selected scene, derived from character name blocks in your script.

## Selecting a character

Click a character name in the cast panel to select their stand-in actor in the viewport. The viewport will focus on that actor.

## Cast vs. crew

The cast panel shows **speaking characters** only — extracted from Character Name blocks in the script. Non-speaking background characters are not listed.

## Coming soon

Character thumbnail images will be added in a future release. For now, the panel displays names only.
